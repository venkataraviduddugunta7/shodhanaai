# Customer Pitch Brief Template

Use this before generating a powerful pitch or PPT for one customer.

## Customer Name

Example: Pfizer

## Customer Type

Example: Formulation company / API buyer / CDMO prospect / distributor / competitor

## Target Product

Example: Duloxetine API

## Region And Market

Example: USA, regulated market

## What They Currently Buy

Add product, estimated volume, price, and supplier if known.

## Why They Are A Good Target

Explain the business reason.

## Shodhana Strengths To Highlight

- Regulatory support
- Quality
- Capacity
- Supply reliability
- Vertical integration

## Risks Or Unknowns

Add missing data, competitor lock-in, regulatory gaps, or pricing issues.

## Desired Output

Example:

- Intro email
- Follow-up email
- 8-slide PPT
- Opportunity note
- Meeting talking points
