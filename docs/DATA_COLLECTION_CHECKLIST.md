# Data Collection Checklist

Use this checklist before the next Shodhana meeting.

## Company Knowledge

- Company profile
- Corporate brochure
- Facility details
- Manufacturing sites
- Capacity details
- Regulatory approvals
- Certifications
- Quality systems
- R&D capability
- CDMO capability
- Approved corporate pitch language

## Duloxetine API Knowledge

- Product name
- CAS number
- Pharmacopeia support
- DMF/CEP details
- DMF submitted countries
- Commercial status
- Capacity
- Batch size
- Key strengths
- Known limitations
- Competitor comparison
- Approved pitch points

## Duloxetine Pellets Knowledge

- Product details
- Strengths
- Regulatory support
- Capacity
- Customer use case
- Commercial status
- Differentiation from API pitch

## Market And Customer Data

- Buyer company
- Region
- Country
- Product bought
- Volume estimate
- Price estimate
- Competitor supplier
- Buying trend
- Existing Shodhana customer or not
- Decision maker/contact, if available
- Notes from sales team

## Pitch Examples

Collect approved examples:

- Best old email introductions
- Best follow-up emails
- Best corporate PPT
- Best product PPT
- Best CDMO pitch
- Best lost customer analysis
- Best successful customer conversion story

These examples are needed later for style and fine-tuning.
